Solutions

Solve the Shared-Device Identity Problem

Traditional IAM platforms are built for 1-person-1-device. Blaufant is built for the real world of shift work, shared fleets, and offline-first environments.

🏭 Shared Device Management

Dozens of workers sharing a pool of rugged handhelds — Blaufant tracks who used which device, when, and what apps they accessed. Full accountability without friction.

🌐 Offline & Disconnected Auth

Cold storage, underground facilities, or remote field work — Blaufant caches credentials securely on-device so workers authenticate even with zero network connectivity.

🔄 Identity Provider Integration

Already using Okta, Azure AD, Ping, or LDAP? Blaufant bridges your existing IdP to rugged devices without replacing your identity stack.

🔒 Zero-Trust Device Security

Every authentication request is verified against device posture, user context, and network environment. Suspicious sessions are automatically blocked or stepped up.

📦 MDM-Agnostic Deployment

Deploy Blaufant on top of any MDM — SOTI, VMware Workspace ONE, Microsoft Intune, or Jamf — without ripping and replacing your existing device management.

Blaufant Architecture

How identity flows from your IdP to the edge device in real time.

1
Worker scans badge / enters PIN on rugged device
2
Blaufant Edge Agent validates local credential cache
3
Cloud sync confirms session with your IdP (Okta / Azure AD)
4
Adaptive MFA triggered if context risk score elevated
5
SSO token issued — apps open instantly, no re-login
6
Full session logged to FleetAudit for compliance

How It Works

Deploy in Days, Not Months

Blaufant is designed to drop into your existing device and identity stack without a rip-and-replace project.

01

Connect Your IdP

Link Blaufant to your existing identity provider — Azure AD, Okta, Ping, or on-prem LDAP — in under 30 minutes using our guided setup wizard.

02

Enroll Your Devices

Push the Blaufant Edge Agent via your existing MDM (SOTI, Intune, Workspace ONE). Bulk enrollment for thousands of devices with zero-touch provisioning.

03

Configure Auth Policies

Define who can log in, with which factors, on which devices. Set offline grace periods, session timeouts, and step-up triggers from the cloud console.

04

Go Live & Monitor

Flip the switch. Workers authenticate instantly. FleetAudit captures every event. Your security team monitors from a single pane of glass.
